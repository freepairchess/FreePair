# FreePair v0.37

This release is a big one for **section setup, tiebreaks, prizes, and
standings** — the tools you reach for at the start and end of an event.

## What's new in v0.37

- **Edit a section after you've created it.** A new **Edit section** dialog
  (the pencil icon in the section list, and a button on the Overview tab)
  lets you fix a section's name, subtitle, time control, number of rounds,
  and its half-point-bye allowance — no need to delete and recreate. Renaming
  a section keeps all its players, pairings, and results intact.

- **Choose your own tiebreaks — and their order.** From the Standings or
  Wall Chart tab, click **Tiebreaks…** to pick exactly which tiebreak systems
  to use and the order they apply. Your choice drives both how the standings
  are sorted *and* which tiebreak columns are shown (on screen and in the
  PDF), and Standings and the Wall Chart always stay in sync. New systems are
  available alongside the classics: **Sonneborn-Berger**, **Median
  (Harkness)**, **Number of Wins**, and **Number of Black Games**. Prefer no
  tiebreaks at all? Deselect them and equal scores are simply ranked by
  rating.

- **Place prizes, calculated for you.** Each section has a **Prizes** tab
  where you enter the cash prizes for 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and so on. When the
  event is over, click **Calculate prizes** and FreePair works out the
  winners **by score** — players tied for a set of places share the pooled
  cash for those places, split evenly (rounded to the cent). The results
  stick around when you reopen the event, clear automatically if you change
  the prizes or delete the last round, and there's a **Clear prizes** button
  whenever you want to start over. Calculate before the event is finished and
  FreePair warns you the results are provisional.

- **See the whole story in the Standings.** The Standings tab now shows each
  player's **round-by-round results** and their **requested byes**, right
  alongside score and tiebreaks — so you can see how every placing was
  earned without switching to the Wall Chart.

- **Requested byes on the Wall Chart.** Upcoming (requested) byes now appear
  on the Wall Chart, and every bye is **color-coded** so full, half, and
  zero-point byes — and byes still to come — stand out at a glance.

- **A backup prompt when you close the app.** Previously the "save a copy to
  the cloud?" prompt only appeared when you closed the *event*. Now closing
  the whole application offers the same backup, so you never miss the chance
  to keep a portable copy.

and the rules-reference document — no additional downloads or configuration
required.

## Known limitations (still planned for later releases)

- **Prizes are place-based for now.** Class / rating-band prizes (e.g. "top
  Under-1800", "top unrated") aren't calculated automatically yet — this
  release covers place prizes. Any class prizes already in an imported file
  are still shown for reference.
- **Prize winners are computed on final scores.** Prizes deliberately ignore
  tiebreaks (matching USCF practice): tied players split the pooled cash for
  the places they cover.
- **Tiebreak selection is per section.** Each section keeps its own tiebreak
  configuration; there is no single event-wide setting yet.
