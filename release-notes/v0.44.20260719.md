# FreePair v0.44.20260719

A UI-consistency pass across the section tabs, plus matching improvements to
the printable PDFs.

## What's new

- **Consistent toolbars on every section tab.** Roster, Pairings, Standings,
  Wall Chart, and Byes & Withdrawals now share one look: the action buttons and
  menus sit on the left inside a single panel, and the **Filter** box is docked
  to the right at a consistent width. When you narrow the window the buttons
  wrap onto a second row instead of being cut off.
- **Cleaner Overview tab.** Section settings are now laid out in a tidy card
  with right-aligned labels (matching the Event screen), and **Pairing Engine**
  and **Pairing Rule** sit together near the top.
- **Full borders on every table.** All grids now draw a complete outer border so
  columns read as a proper table.
- **Byes & Withdrawals shows upcoming byes too.** A single list now combines
  byes that have already happened with **requested future byes** for rounds that
  aren't paired yet. Upcoming byes are highlighted in green so they're easy to
  spot at a glance.
- **Publish from the Pairings tab.** A new **Publish Pairing & Results to NACH**
  action is available in the Pairing Operations menu. It reminds you that
  publishing covers the whole event (every section), then runs the same publish
  step as the Event menu.
- **Prints now match what you see on screen.** The **Standings** and **Wall
  Chart** PDFs include every scheduled round (with upcoming byes highlighted),
  the **Wall Chart** PDF now shows tiebreak columns like Standings, and the
  **Byes** PDF uses the same combined, highlighted list as the tab.
- **Roster print protects player privacy.** The printed player roster now shows
  exactly the columns you've chosen to display — **except birth date, email, and
  phone, which are never printed**. This keeps those personal details off a
  roster that may be posted on the wall in the tournament hall.

## Installer

The Windows installer bundles the .NET runtime — download, run, and FreePair
self-updates on future releases.
