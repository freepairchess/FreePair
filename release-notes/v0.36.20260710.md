# FreePair v0.36

This release makes it easier
in this version, to **copy** a player into a second section so they can
appear in two places at once.

## What's new in v0.36

- **A "Move" button now sits right on the Players tab.** Moving one or
  more players into another section no longer means hunting for the
  section side-menu — the Players toolbar has a new **↔ Move** button next
  to Add / Import / Print. Click it, tick the players, choose the
  destination (an existing section or a brand-new one), and go. If the
  destination has already paired rounds, you still choose what to record
  for each missed round (full / half / zero-point bye), exactly as before.

- **Copy a player into a second section — they stay where they are.** The
  move dialog gained a **"Copy (keep players in the source section too)"**
  option. Tick it and the selected players remain in their current section
  *and* are added to the destination. This is perfect for situations where
  a player needs to be in more than one section at once — for example,
  dropping a player who took an allocated bye into a **"Side Games"**
  section so they still get a game, without pulling them out of their main
  event. Because a copy never disturbs the original section, you can copy a
  player even after that section has started pairing (a plain *move* still
  needs to happen before round 1, since it renumbers the roster).

- **Cleaner event-toolbar labels.** The event action buttons were tidied to
  consistent Title Case ("Sync All Rosters with NACH", "All Pairings in
  Windows", "Export USCF Report", …) so the toolbar reads uniformly.

## Known limitations (still planned for later releases)

- **A player copied into a second section is an independent copy.** Editing
  their rating, name, or results in one section does not update the other —
  the two entries live in their own sections from the moment you copy. This
  is by design (each section pairs and scores independently); just re-copy
  or edit both if you need them re-synced.
- **A plain move still requires a pre-round-1 source.** Physically moving a
  player out of a section renumbers that section, which is only safe before
  round 1 is paired. Once a section has paired, use **Copy** (allowed any
  time) or withdraw the player instead.
- **SwissSys fidelity is unchanged from v0.35.** This release adds a
  roster-management convenience and does not change how any section pairs.
